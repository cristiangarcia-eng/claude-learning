**Status:** Needs refresh — several data points are 3+ months old

## Nike Strengths

- Strongest brand recognition globally (aided awareness: 96% in US)
- Deepest athlete endorsement roster across all major sports
- Leading DTC digital platform (Nike App, SNKRS)
- Innovation pipeline: Flyknit, Air Max, Vaporfly, ZoomX
- Scale advantage in manufacturing and distribution

## Nike Weaknesses

- Premium pricing limits growth in emerging markets
- Heavy North America dependence (40% of revenue)
- Inventory management challenges in 2024-2025
- Declining cultural relevance among Gen Z (losing ground to New Balance and Asics in lifestyle)
- Sustainability messaging perceived as less authentic than Allbirds or On Running

## Competitor Highlights (as of Jan 2026)

### Adidas
- Samba and Gazelle retro trend drove strong lifestyle sales in 2025
- Running category still weak vs. Nike and On Running
- New CEO restructuring DTC strategy
- Sustainability: Parley ocean plastic line, but not a core brand message

### New Balance
- Fastest growth among legacy brands (11.3% YoY)
- Cultural relevance surging — collaborations with designers and celebrities
- Made in USA line commanding premium pricing
- Running credibility growing with FuelCell technology

### On Running
- Premium running market leader in several European markets
- Cyclon subscription model (fully recyclable shoe) getting press attention
- Expanding into lifestyle and tennis categories
- Small but aggressive: 18.7% growth rate

### Hoka
- Dominant in ultra-running and maximalist cushion segment
- Expanding rapidly into casual/walking market
- Strong word-of-mouth in healthcare and hospitality (all-day comfort)
- Fastest-growing brand in the space (22.4% YoY)

## Key Trends to Watch

1. **Sustainability arms race** — consumers increasingly expect eco claims backed by evidence
2. **Retro/lifestyle dominance** — running shoes worn as fashion, not just sport
3. **DTC vs. wholesale tension** — brands pulling from retailers, then returning
4. **China recovery** — slow but improving, local brands gaining ground
5. **AI personalization** — Nike App using AI for product recommendations, competitors following
