## Notes

- Start with 1-2 URLs. You do not need to fetch every page.
- Some pages may return limited content or be blocked — that is normal.
- Focus on what competitors are currently promoting vs. what our snapshot says.
- Ask Claude to look for specific things: pricing, new product names, sustainability claims, featured technologies.
