# Project Management Tools — Landscape Report

**Prepared for:** Operations Team
**Date:** March 2025

---

## Recommendation

**For a 50-person company with mixed technical and non-technical users: Linear** for engineering workflows + **Notion** for cross-functional collaboration. Total cost: ~$800/month.

If budget requires a single tool: **Asana** offers the best balance of simplicity for non-technical users and enough power for technical teams.

### Pros & Cons

**Asana**
- Pros: Intuitive for everyone, strong portfolios, good integrations
- Cons: Gets expensive at scale, reporting could be deeper

**Linear**
- Pros: Blazing fast, developers love it, clean design
- Cons: Opinionated (no Gantt), less suitable for non-technical teams alone

**Notion**
- Pros: Flexible, great for docs + light project management
- Cons: Can become messy without structure, not a dedicated PM tool
