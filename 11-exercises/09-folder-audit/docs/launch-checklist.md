# Launch Checklist: Team Workspaces

## Pre-Launch (1 week before GA)

- [ ] QA sign-off on all critical paths
- [ ] Load testing complete (target: 500 concurrent workspace users)
- [ ] Beta feedback reviewed and P0 issues resolved
- [ ] Help center articles published
- [ ] In-app tooltip tour created for first-time workspace users
- [ ] Email announcement drafted and approved by Marketing
- [ ] Pricing page updated to reflect workspace limits by plan

## Launch Day

- [ ] Feature flag flipped to 100%
- [ ] Blog post published
- [ ] Email sent to all Pro and Enterprise customers
- [ ] Social media posts scheduled
- [ ] Support team briefed with FAQ document
- [ ] Monitor error rates for first 2 hours

## Post-Launch (first 2 weeks)

- [ ] Daily check on adoption metrics
- [ ] Support ticket volume tracking
- [ ] Collect NPS from beta users
- [ ] Schedule retro for launch team
- [ ] Begin Phase 2 scoping based on feedback
